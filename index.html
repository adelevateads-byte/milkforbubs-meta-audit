Gaps observed

⚙️

No real prospecting → retargeting split

Only 3 campaigns exist (1 paused). 77% of spend sits in "Prospecting Campaign" (6.22× ROAS) while a smaller campaign gets 22% at a better 8.09× ROAS. Meta's own opportunity score flags scaling the smaller winner as a +40% conversion opportunity.

📡

Campaigns are warm/hot, not actually cold

Inclusions and exclusions mean both "prospecting" campaigns are really running on CRM- and purchaser-seeded lookalikes — warm-audience logic wearing a cold-prospecting label. It shows up in delivery: the Prospecting Campaign's reach sits at only ~31,000–36,000 against ~S$3,700–3,900/month spend, pushing frequency to 8.65–11.5× — high for what's meant to be cold prospecting, and a direct result of the audience being narrower than the label suggests. Worth revisiting whether "prospecting" should mean genuinely cold, interest-only or broad targeting instead.

📱

Facebook Reels placement underperforming — but it's Advantage+

S$973.74 spent at 3.60× ROAS, 45% below the account's blended average. But the campaigns run Advantage+, which locks placement selection — testing a manual placement exclusion means giving up Advantage+ automation for that ad set. Given Reels is only ~3% of spend, worth watching for a few weeks before disrupting automation for a manual test.

🖇️

Engagement is fragmenting across duplicate posts

The same creative is being uploaded fresh for each ad rather than sharing one post ID, so likes, comments and shares split across multiple copies instead of accumulating on one — diluting the social proof each ad could be building.

🎯

Creative isn't matched to funnel stage

The same creative mix runs regardless of whether the audience is cold or warm. Reviews and testimonials belong in retargeting, where trust-building converts; different hooks are needed for cold prospecting. Right now the funnel stage doesn't change the content.

🧪

No structured destination testing

No consistent A/B testing across where traffic lands — product page vs. collection vs. homepage vs. dedicated landing page.

📊

Measurement visibility is incomplete

Pixel health and Shopify-side data can't be fully cross-checked with the current access level. A full-depth measurement audit needs either broader Meta access or direct Shopify/GA4 access.

Five areas addressed concurrently — directly against what you asked for: a structure that scales, and one your team can run.

Campaign strategy

Rebuild the funnel

Restructure from the ground up so cold and warm audiences are actually doing different jobs.

  • Build true cold prospecting (broad / interest-only, no CRM seeding) feeding a distinct warm retargeting layer
  • Reallocate budget toward the higher-ROAS Premium Mommy Starter Kit campaign
🎬

Creative management

Fix rotation and funnel matching

Make sure the right message reaches the right audience, and nothing goes to waste.

  • Set up shared post IDs so the same creative accumulates engagement instead of fragmenting across duplicates
  • Map creative to funnel stage — reviews and testimonials in retargeting, distinct hooks for cold prospecting
🧪

Placement & testing

Structured testing program

Stop guessing where budget and traffic perform best.

  • Monitor Facebook Reels performance for a few weeks before considering a manual placement test against Advantage+
  • Run a destination testing calendar across PDP, collection, homepage and landing page
  • Document what wins per campaign type as a reusable playbook
